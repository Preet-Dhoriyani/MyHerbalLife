1. Formula 1 Nutritional Shake Mix

Mix 25g (2 scoops) of Formula 1 Shake with 250ml of cold water or skimmed milk.
Blend/shake well until smooth.
Consume as a meal replacement once or twice a day for healthy weight management.

2. ShakeMate

Add 1 scoop of ShakeMate to your Formula 1 Shake for improved taste and creamy texture.
Mix and consume immediately.

3. Personalized Protein Powder

Add 1 to 2 scoops of protein powder to your Formula 1 shake or any beverage.
Helps meet daily protein requirement based on body weight.

4. Enhancer Tablets

Take 1 tablet of each enhancer (like Multivitamin, Cell-U-Loss, Herbal Control etc.) after meals.
Do not exceed the recommended dosage.
Consult your wellness coach for personalized plan.

5. Afresh Energy Drink

Mix 1 gram (½ teaspoon) of Afresh powder in 160ml hot or cold water.
Drink it in the morning or before workouts for a boost of energy and metabolism support.
Can be consumed 1–2 times per day.
